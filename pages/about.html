About Aleena

Hi! I'm Aleena Kanner. I grew up on Long Island, where I attended Great Neck South High School. I was an active member of the Gymnastics team for four years, and is where my passion for all things fitness originated. I got my bachelors degree at SUNY Cortland, and then went on to get my Masters in Athletic Training at the University of Arkansas (Go Hogs!).
