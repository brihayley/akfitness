Cupping Therapy

Cupping therapy is a type of soft tissue treatment used to alleviate muscular tightness or soreness. Can also be used for stress, pain, allergies, fatigue, flu, colds, back pain, anxiety, muscle aches, red itchy skin conditions or fever.
